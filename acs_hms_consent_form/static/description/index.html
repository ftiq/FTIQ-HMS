Electronic Consent Form for Hospital Management System

This module enhances consent form management by allowing hospitals to create, sign, and maintain a history of digitally signed documents. Hospitals frequently require consent forms from patients for various procedures, and this module streamlines the process with a structured, trackable system.

Benefits of Electronic Consent Form

Predefined Consent Form Templates for quick processing with structured forms, lists, and search options.

Integrated Chatter in Consent Forms to facilitate communication and record discussions.

Generate and print both standard and signed consent forms seamlessly.

Dedicated user roles for managing the consent form workflow efficiently.

Smart button on partner records to access all related consent forms instantly.

Customers can view and electronically sign consent forms directly from the portal.
