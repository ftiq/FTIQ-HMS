Hospital Management System for Nephrology

This module is designed to manage nephrology departments efficiently, covering dialysis sessions, patient records, vascular access types, dialyzer and dialysate tracking, and nephrology-specific procedures within Odoo.

Module Features

Nephrology patients receiving only Nephrology care.
Nephrology department appointments.
Nephrology department treatment.
Nephrology department patient procedure.

Nephrology Menu View

Appointment Nephrology Procedure

Etiology of Chronic Renal Failure: Identifies underlying causes contributing to progressive kidney function decline.
Way of Arrival: Tracks patient arrival methods like walk-in, ambulance, or referral.
Adverse Reactions: Documents any negative effects to ensure patient safety.
Transplant Waiting List: Tracks transplant list status for timely updates.
Toxic Habits: Records habits like smoking or alcohol for personalized plans.

Patient Nephrology Procedure

Hemodialysis Medication: Heparin, bicarbonate, erythropoietin, and other medications used during treatment.
Electrolyte Levels: Potassium, sodium and calcium levels monitored during dialysis.
Blood Culture Testing for infections in the blood during dialysis.
Treatment Details Information about the actual dialysis treatment, including blood flow and dialysis fluid flow.

Manage different types of vascular access for dialysis.

Manage and define the types of dialyzers used in nephrology treatments.

Manage various types of dialysate solutions for dialysis procedures.

Manage patient race information for nephrology records.

Set and manage the nephrology treatment schedule for patients.
